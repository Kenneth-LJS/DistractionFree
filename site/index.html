Focus on the writing

The distraction free mode for Google Docs hides all controls and buttons and lets you focus on the writing.

Tip: Check out the Google Docs keyboard shortcuts for the things you do the most and you never have to switch back again.

Themes for every situation

Pick the style that suits you, whether it's the dark mode suitable for late night prose, sepia for caffeine-fueled poems, or the clean gray default gray theme. More are planned for the future.

Tip: Make your browser full-screen for an even better distraction free
